Meet SakshiYoga

Nobody can deny the need for physical, mental and spiritual wellbeing in this fast and uptight life. Practicing Yoga helps you meet all this criteria.

We believe wellness begins long before you step onto a yoga mat and continues long after you leave it. Through mindful movement, breath, and awareness, we help you cultivate flexibility, reconnect with your body, and feel both free and grounded. Wherever you are in your journey, Sakshi Yoga meets you there and together, we'll move towards a healthier, more balanced life.

Common Concerns

Transparent answers for your wellness journey.

I've never done yoga before. Is this for me?

expand_more
Absolutely. Most of my students start exactly where you are. We prioritize slow, explained movements and offer modifications for every body type.

What if I have limited mobility or chronic pain?

expand_more
Our sessions are highly adaptive. We use props and modifications to ensure every movement is safe and supportive for your specific body needs.

Do I need to be flexible to start?

expand_more
Not at all. Yoga is the tool to build flexibility, not a prerequisite. We focus on functional movement that meets you exactly where you are.

Can we practice yoga during menstrual phase?

expand_more
No, we need to take rest and enjoy with awareness. You can resume yoga from fifth day  of your menstruation.

Do you offer one-on-one sessions?

expand_more
Yes, specifically for health concerns like PCOD or recovery. You can book a consultation to discuss a personalized plan.
